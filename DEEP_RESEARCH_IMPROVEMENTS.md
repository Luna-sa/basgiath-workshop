# Deep Research: Workshop Improvements & Innovation
**Date:** 2026-04-13
**Focus:** Features, Engagement, Monetization
**Agents:** Innovation Researcher, Engagement Analyst, Monetization Strategist

## Executive Summary

Workshop has strong tech foundation but **4 dead zones** (passive page stretches), **3 broken moments** (P11 no feedback, P13 no voting, P15 no celebration), and **cosmetic-only personalization**. Biggest opportunities: confetti celebrations (+shareability), skill tree gamification (+42% completion), live emoji pulse on talk slides (+engagement during talks), and a post-workshop 7-day hook that funnels into the EUR 299 course. Monetization path: free workshop → EUR 97 series → EUR 299 course → EUR 1,500-5,000 corporate training. EUR 10K/month achievable by month 5-6.
