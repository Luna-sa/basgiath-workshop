## Style rules

- **Tone:** peer-style "ти" form in UK and RU. The workshop is not
  corporate.
- **Apostrophe in UK:** use the proper apostrophe `'` in words like
  рев'ю, м'ясо, ім'я, зв'язок. Not `'`.
- **Don't transliterate Russian into Ukrainian.** Translate the
  meaning. E.g.
  - RU "соревнование" → UA "змагання" (not "соревнування")
  - RU "перевод" → UA "переклад" (not "перевод")
  - RU "выбор" → UA "вибір" (not "вибор")
- **Keep English technical terms** untranslated: CLAUDE.md, MCP,
  Whisper, Playwright, Supabase, Render, Telegram, Discord,
  iMessage, Spotify, gpt-image-2, subagent, skill, autopilot.
- **Avoid calques.** Read your output aloud — if a Russian speaker
  would notice the structure is "translated", rewrite it.
