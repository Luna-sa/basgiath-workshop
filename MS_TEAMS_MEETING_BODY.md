**Use this** as the body of your Teams calendar invite (the long-form
description that recipients see in Outlook before joining). MS Teams
meetings strip most HTML, so this is plain text with light formatting
that holds up everywhere.

## How to use in Outlook / Teams

### Option A: Outlook desktop → New Teams Meeting

1. Outlook → New Items → Teams Meeting
2. Subject: `QA Clan · Claude Code Workshop`
3. Required attendees: paste the registered list (or use a distribution list)
4. Date / time: May 13, 2026, 14:00 - 15:30
5. Location: `Watermelon room or Teams`
6. Body: paste the plain-text block above (Cmd+A to select, Cmd+C, then paste in the body)
7. Click Send

The Teams join link is added automatically by Outlook above your text.

### Option B: Teams calendar directly

1. Teams → Calendar → New meeting
2. Title: `QA Clan · Claude Code Workshop`
3. Date / time / attendees
4. Location: `Watermelon room`
5. Details (the body field at the bottom): paste the plain-text block
